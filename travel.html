Travel & Accommodations

Everything you need to plan your trip to Minneapolis.

Getting Here

✈

By Air

The closest airport is Minneapolis–Saint Paul International Airport (MSP), located about 15 miles from the venues. Most major airlines fly into MSP with both domestic and international connections.

MSP Airport Website →

🚗

By Car

Minneapolis is easily accessible from I-94, I-35W, and I-494. Parking is available at both the ceremony and reception venues at no charge. See the venue addresses on our Schedule page.

🚋

Getting Around

  • Uber & Lyft are widely available
  • Metro Transit light rail from MSP airport to downtown
  • Complimentary shuttle between venues on the wedding day
  • Taxi services available citywide
