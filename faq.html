The dress code is Black Tie Optional. We encourage you to dress up and celebrate with us! Floral prints and romantic colors are warmly welcomed. Please avoid white, ivory, and champagne out of respect for the bride.

Yes! Free parking is available at both the ceremony and reception venues. We also recommend using ride-share services like Uber or Lyft so everyone can enjoy the celebration fully. See our Travel page for more details.

Yes! We will have a complimentary shuttle running between the ceremony and reception venues, and back to the partner hotels at the end of the night. The shuttle schedule will be shared closer to the date.

June in Minneapolis is typically warm and beautiful, with average temperatures around 70–80°F (21–27°C). Evenings can be a bit cooler, so you may want to bring a light wrap or jacket for after the outdoor cocktail hour. Occasional thunderstorms are possible, so keep an eye on the forecast!
