Things To Do

Our favorite spots in Minneapolis — hand-picked just for you.

🍴

Spoon and Stable

Gavin Kaysen's flagship restaurant in the North Loop. Exceptional French-American cuisine in a beautiful converted warehouse space. Reservations highly recommended.

🍴 Fine Dining · North Loop
☕

Parlour Bar

A lively cocktail bar in the North Loop with creative drinks and a cozy atmosphere. Perfect for a pre-dinner cocktail or a nightcap.

☕ Cocktails · North Loop
🍲

Nighthawks

A classic American diner-style spot open late. Great burgers, milkshakes, and breakfast all day. A local favorite for a casual bite.

🍲 Casual · Uptown
🍺

Indeed Brewing

One of Minneapolis's favorite craft breweries with a fantastic taproom and rotating seasonal beers. Great for a relaxed afternoon.

🍺 Brewery · NE Minneapolis
🎨

Minneapolis Institute of Art

World-class art museum with over 90,000 works spanning 5,000 years. Free general admission. A must-visit for art lovers.

🎨 Museum · Free
🏥

First Avenue & 7th St Entry

Legendary music venue immortalized by Prince. Check the calendar for shows happening during your visit — the energy here is unmatched.

🎵 Live Music · Downtown
🛍

Nicollet Mall

Minneapolis's main pedestrian-friendly shopping street with boutiques, restaurants, and street art. Great for a leisurely afternoon stroll.

🛍 Shopping · Downtown
🏠

Northeast Arts District

The NE Minneapolis arts scene is thriving with galleries, studios, and creative restaurants. The Art-A-Whirl studio tour happens every May — just before the wedding!

🎨 Arts · NE Minneapolis
🌿

Chain of Lakes

A stunning connected network of lakes — Calhoun, Harriet, Isles, and Cedar — with scenic paths for walking, running, and biking. A Minneapolis gem.

🌿 Outdoors · Southwest Mpls
🌊

Minnehaha Falls

A beautiful 53-foot waterfall within Minnehaha Regional Park. Walk the trails, have a picnic, and enjoy some of the most scenic nature in the city.

🌊 Nature · Free
🚴

Nice Ride Bike Share

Rent a bike and explore Minneapolis's 100+ miles of bike lanes. The city is incredibly bike-friendly — a great way to see the lakes and neighborhoods.

🚴 Biking · Citywide
🏭

Minnesota Zoo

One of the country's top zoos with over 4,500 animals and beautiful natural habitats. Located in Apple Valley, about 20 minutes south of Minneapolis.

🏭 Family · Apple Valley
🔭

Science Museum of Minnesota

Interactive exhibits spanning science, technology, and natural history. Located in St. Paul, right next to the Mississippi River. Great for all ages.

🔭 Museum · St. Paul
🎣

Mall of America

The largest mall in the US, complete with an indoor theme park, aquarium, mini-golf, and hundreds of shops. Located 15 minutes from downtown.

🎣 Shopping · Bloomington
